Hi! My name is Mehdi Pilehvarian, a Toronto-based front-end web developer and thinker who loves creative challenges. As a former film/video editor, I'm resiliant, a creative problem solver, and well-acquainted with collaboration and iteration. I'm a graduate of Juno College of Technology's immersive coding bootcamp.

I'm passionate about building creative and original websites as well as interactive apps! I'm self-motivated and curious life-long learner, who is excited for challenges that come with coding and new technologies. I strive to create accessible digital products that bring delightful and seamless user experience. I enjoy coding in Javascript, React and Sass.
